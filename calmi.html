Calmi

Gothenburg I October 2024

Calmi is an app designed to help users manage anxiety through a combination of AI-assisted support, ICE (In Case of Emergency) contacts for urgent help, and an anxiety journal. It allows users to reflect on their emotions, analyze data such as heart rate and the number of anxiety episodes, and perform relaxation exercises. The app focuses on fostering self-awareness and providing practical tools to manage everyday stress.
