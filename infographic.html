Infographic

Gothenburg I September 2024

For a school project, I illustrated and described myself through an infographic. I chose to highlight my passion for TV series and presented six shows that I rewatch every year. The infographic displayed the number of times I’ve watched each series, along with a personal rating from 0-5. By illustrating the series’ covers, viewers could quickly recognize them, making the information both visual and engaging. It was a creative way to combine data and personal insights!
